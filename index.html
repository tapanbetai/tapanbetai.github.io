About me

 

By Education, I am an Engineer who is highly influenced by active members of maker community all around. I started my journey of making from my college life which lead me to technical expert and instructor at FabLab CEPT, Ahmedabad. I always loved teaching here I am teaching, discussing, guiding, mentoring not just students but professionals, tech enthusiastic, children also.

 

My journey at fablab cept started from November 2014, When Henry (Initiator of FabLabCEPT) conducted a workshop on CNC milling. In my college life I was working on my STEM education initiative and it came in my knowledge that

'Makerfest' one of the initiative associated with fablab CEPT needs help and

after getting partnered with Makerfest, I started visiting fablab regularly and I

found really interesting environment; intelligent, intellectual, knowledgeable,

geek people around the lab. Found people working on some serious projects, some were having fun by making things to learn, some were making something for fun. The Environment around the Lab attracted me to visit it again after the event, then I became a regular member of the lab and then I started learning about the machines around simultaneously working on my projects. Henry, Parth, Vipul took my knowledge of fabrication from scratch to a certain level and then I was offered Fab-Academy 2016 course from FabLab CEPT which i took without thinking for a moment. I finished my B.tech and Fab-Academy course at the same time and I was offered an opportunity of working for FabLabCEPT as a Technical expert and Instructor.
